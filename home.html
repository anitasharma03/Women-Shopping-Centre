About Shoppers


Shoopers was launched in 2006 and have been your fashion bestie ever since. Since those modest beginnings, this publicly owned, family-controlled business has evolved from one women’s clothing store on Boulevard St. Laurent in Montreal to one of Canada’s largest women’s specialty retailer.Style never sleeps and neither do we – we're 24/7, dropping over 500 new products a week, so you always have the latest looks for less. We make fashion accessible and fun, so every girl can get the wardrobe of her dreams.One thing we love more than your amazing style is your individuality – we want to help you show it off, do your thing and look incredible at the same time.
